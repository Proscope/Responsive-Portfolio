About Me

I graduated from Troy University with a Bachelors in Applied Computer Science. I also attended Georgia College and State University, where I played tennis. Tennis has always been one of my biggest passions growing up. I worked at a tennis specialty retail store for over a decade because of this passion. I have recently left that job to pursue my other passion of Web development.

I am currently enrolled in a coding bootcamp to help further my knowledge and skills in Web development. I also am married and have an eight month old son. My wife is a teacher and the best person I know. I never knew how becoming a father would change my life but now he just lights up my life.
